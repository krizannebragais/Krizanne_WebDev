Nostalgia's Embrace

I captured this scene near my old elementary school in the afternoon light. The park and the old building stand side by side, returning me to my school days. The building has been there for ages. On the right, there's a busy road with cars rushing by. It's a sharp contrast to the quiet park and the old building, reminding me that time keeps moving forward. This picture makes me feel nostalgic, thinking about the good times I had in that park and at my old school. It's a mix of old memories and the bustling life of today.

Sunset Serenity

As the sun set, I took this picture. The trees looked like dark outlines against the colorful sky. Below, the sea was calm, with small boats floating peacefully. It was a really pretty and peaceful moment, and I wanted to capture it.

Manila Bay Nights

I took this shot at MOA Seaside during the night. The MOA Eye, a giant ferris wheel, stood beside Manila Bay, lit up with colorful lights. The reflection on the bay's calm waters added to the magic. Surrounding it, a sea of tall buildings glittered with lights, and cars buzzed through the streets below. It was a breathtaking urban spectacle, and I couldn't resist capturing the brilliance of the MOA Eye against the backdrop of the city's vibrant lights.

BGC Buzz

I was on top of a building in BGC, and the scene below was incredible. The streets were jam- packed with cars, and right in the middle was this beautiful fountain shooting water into the air. It was a striking contrast: the busy streets and the serene fountain. I was so amazed by the hustle and bustle of the city that I had to snap this picture. It's like a snapshot of the city's heartbeat and always leaves me in awe.

Majestic Mayon

I found myself in Tamaoyan, gazing at the magnificent Mayon Volcano during the afternoon. Although I yearned to witness the fiery lava flow, the bright daylight obscured its presence.

Okada Manila

I was at Okada Manila, enjoying the mesmerizing "The Fountain" performance under the night sky. As I watched, my attention shifted to the hotel rooms nearby. Some were bathed in warm, inviting light, while others remained in complete darkness. It was a captivating sight, this contrast between the illuminated rooms and the ones enveloped in darkness.
